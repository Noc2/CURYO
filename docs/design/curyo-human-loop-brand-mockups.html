Reusable system

Faceless Orbs With State

Use the same black core, white structure, and orange orbit language everywhere avatars appear. The orbit density and shade can communicate role or status without adding cute facial features.

  • AI agents: white capsule face with the orange C signal inside.
  • Humans: human signal mark inside the same orb container.
  • Pools: concentric orange rings for aggregated judgment.
  • Pending or low-confidence states: dimmer orange and dotted orbit treatment.

Suggested Direction

The proposed refresh is the best low-risk path because it keeps the current landing architecture while making the first impression much clearer and warmer. Manrope makes the page feel less poster-like than Bebas Neue while preserving product clarity.

Prompt Lock

For further image edits: preserve the preferred keyboard-hand raster composition, keep pure white strokes, keep the mug, use only orange-family AI orbit accents, and avoid golden linework or detailed anatomy.
